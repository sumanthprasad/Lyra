About Lyra

Emotion-aware, feedback-driven recommendations.

Lyra blends lyric sentiment, audio features, and your real-time feedback to keep the queue on your vibe. Built with Flask, a GAN-based recommender, and Spotify data, it pivots instantly when you like, skip, or correct the mood.

What Lyra does

  • Ingests any Spotify playlist and extracts audio features like tempo, energy, valence, and danceability.
  • Classifies lyric emotion across 13 Berkeley categories to anchor the mood signal.
  • Generates recommendations with a GAN-based model and cosine similarity over curated features.
  • Adapts in-session using explicit feedback (like/dislike, pivot, skip reasons) and implicit cues (replays, fast skips, playthroughs).

How to use it

  1. Drop a playlist URL on the home screen and start a live session.
  2. Hit Mood match, Not it, Pivot closer, or log skip reasons as you listen.
  3. Correct the detected emotion if we got it wrong to steer the next picks.
  4. Watch the queue refresh instantly with your adjustments baked in.

Tech stack

  • Backend: Flask + PostgreSQL (extensible), sessioned feedback APIs.
  • Model: GAN-based recommender with cosine re-ranking over audio/lyric features.
  • Data: Spotify audio features + lyric NLP for emotion labeling.
  • UI: Bootstrap + custom theming with live feedback controls.
